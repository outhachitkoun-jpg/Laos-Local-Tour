Kuang Si Falls, Elephant Walk & Sunset Cruise

8.5 Hours Immersion into a Living Ecosystem

Tour Overview

✨ Experience Highlights

  • Ethical Elephant Encounter: Walk with gentle elephants on a forest trail with their mahouts to a hidden spring.
  • Spring-Side Lunch: Enjoy an authentic Lao lunch at a secluded Khmu restaurant by the spring.
  • Explore Kuang Si Waterfalls: Descend to swim in turquoise pools and explore jungle trails.
  • Heritage Rice Farm: See how the waterfall water sustains local agriculture.
  • Mekong Sunset Cruise: Conclude your day with a serene boat trip on the Mekong River.

Where Water Meets Forest, and Elephants Meet People. Go beyond mere sightseeing on this full-day immersion into the living ecosystem of Laos. This intimate, small-group tour takes you into the hidden forests behind Kuang Si Waterfalls for a respectful journey with elephants.

Quick Info

  • Time: 08:30 AM - 18:30 PM (Full Day)
  • Suitable For: Active travelers & families (Ages 4+)
  • Meals: Fresh Khmu Spring-side Lunch included

Daily Itinerary

08:30 AM

Hotel Pickup & Journey

Start your day with a 40-minute drive into the countryside towards the Kuang Si area.

Morning

Meet the Elephants & Forest Walk

Begin your adventure on a forest trail. Meet gentle elephants and their mahouts. Walk beside them through the forest and learn about the deep bond between Laotians and elephants.

12:00 PM

Lunch at the Hidden Spring

Your trail concludes at a natural spring—the source of the waterfalls. Enjoy an authentic Lao lunch at a secluded Khmu family restaurant.

Afternoon

From Source to Falls

Descend to the main park for 2.5 – 3 hours of free time to swim in the famous turquoise pools.

05:00 PM

Sunset Cruise on the Mekong

Board a traditional boat at the Heritage Rice Farm for a serene cruise as the sun sets over the mountains, returning to town by 6:30 PM.
