An Immersion into a Living Ecosystem

Where Water Meets Forest, and Elephants Meet People.

Go beyond mere sightseeing on this full-day immersion into the living ecosystem of Laos. This intimate, small-group tour takes you into the hidden forests behind Kuang Si Waterfalls for a respectful journey with elephants, walking to a secluded spring. Follow the path of water from this source to the stunning turquoise pools, then to a heritage rice farm, and finally to the timeless Mekong River.

✨ Experience Highlights

  • 🐘 Ethical Elephant Encounter: Walk with gentle elephants on a forest trail, learning from their mahouts as you journey to a hidden spring.
  • 🍽️ Secluded Spring-Side Lunch: Enjoy an authentic Lao lunch at a hidden, open-air Khmu restaurant nestled by the beautiful spring that feeds the waterfalls.
  • 🌊 Explore Kuang Si Waterfalls: Descend to the main park to swim in the iconic turquoise pools and explore jungle trails.
  • 🌾 Heritage Rice Farm: See how the waterfall's water sustains local agriculture at the Kuang Si Heritage Rice Farm.
  • 🌅 Mekong Sunset Cruise: Conclude your day with a serene boat trip on the majestic Mekong River.

Daily Itinerary

08:30 AM - Hotel Pickup & Journey

Start your day with a 40-minute drive into the countryside towards the Kuang Si area.

AM - Meet the Elephants & Forest Walk

Instead of the main gate, begin your adventure on a forest trail. Meet the gentle elephants and their mahouts. Embark on an unforgettable journey through the forest, feeling their gentle rhythm and learning about the deep cultural bond between Laotians and elephants.

12:00 PM - Lunch at the Hidden Spring

Your trail concludes at a beautiful natural spring—the source of the waterfalls. Enjoy an authentic Lao lunch at a secluded restaurant run by a local Khmu family.

PM - From Source to Falls

Take a short hike to the top of the main cascade to see where the water begins its plunge. Then, descend to the park for 2.5 – 3 hours of free time to swim in the famous turquoise pools.

05:00 PM - Sunset Cruise on the Mekong

Board a traditional boat at the Heritage Rice Farm for a serene cruise as the sun sets over the mountains, returning you to Luang Prabang by 6:30 PM.
