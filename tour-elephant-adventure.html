Manifa Elephant Camp & Adventure

Full Day Ethical Encounter & Waterfall Trek

Tour Overview

✨ Highlights

  • Ethical Connection: Walk with elephants in the vast forest near Kuang Si Falls.
  • Kuang Si Falls: Explore the cascading turquoise pools and hidden rice paddies.
  • Sunset Cruise: Relax on a scenic Mekong boat trip back to Luang Prabang.

Manifa Elephant Camp offers a rare opportunity to connect with Laos’ cultural heritage and majestic elephants in a natural riverside setting. We prioritize ethical coexistence, cultural respect, and environmental sustainability, ensuring the well-being of the elephants through responsible care.

Quick Info

  • Duration: Full Day (08:30 AM - 18:30 PM)
  • Experience: Ethical interaction & Forest walking
  • Meals: Fresh Local Buffet Lunch included
