Luang Prabang City Tour

Full Day • Temples, Heritage & Culture

Tour Overview

✨ Highlights

  • 🔸 Royal Palace: Explore the former king's residence and national museum.
  • 🔸 Wat Xieng Thong: The most beautiful and historic temple in Luang Prabang.
  • 🔸 Wat Mai & Wat Visoun: Discover the unique architecture of ancient Buddhist temples.
  • 🔸 Phousi Hill: Climb the 328 steps for a stunning panoramic sunset view of the city and rivers.

Discover the heart and soul of Laos in Luang Prabang, a UNESCO World Heritage site known for its outstanding cultural preservation. This full-day guided city tour taking you through ancient temples, colonial architecture, and the rich history of the former Lane Xang kingdom.

Itinerary

08:30 AM - Morning Temples & Royal Palace

Start your day with a visit to the Royal Palace Museum, which hosts a range of interesting artifacts. Next, head to the magnificent Wat Xieng Thong with its roofs sweeping low to the ground, which represents classical Laotian architecture.

10:30 AM - Historic Wats

Visit Wat Mai and Wat Visoun, the oldest temple in the city, known for its iconic Watermelon Stupa.

12:00 PM - Lunch Break

Free time to grab lunch at a local restaurant by the Mekong river. (Lunch is at your own expense).

02:00 PM - Culture & Craft

Visit the Traditional Arts and Ethnology Centre (TAEC) to learn about the diverse ethnic groups of Laos and their traditional crafts.

04:00 PM - Phousi Hill Sunset

Climb the 328 steps of Mount Phousi for an incredible panoramic view of the city and watch the sunset over the Mekong River. After descending, you have the option to explore the famous Night Market.
