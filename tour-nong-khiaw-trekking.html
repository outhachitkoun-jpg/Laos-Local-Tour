Nong Khiaw: 2-Day Trekking, Camping & Kayaking

2 Days / 1 Night Phakao Peak, Muang Ngoi & Nam Ou River

Tour Overview

✨ Highlights

  • Breathtaking Views: Trek to Phakao Peak for panoramic views of surrounding mountains.
  • Night Under Stars: Enjoy a traditional Laotian dinner and sleep under the stars or in a tent.
  • Remote Villages: Cruise up the Nam Ou River to the blissful remote village of Muang Ngoi.
  • Nam Ou Kayaking: Kayak along the Ou River, taking in views of the jungle and wildlife.

Begin an extraordinary 2-day journey deep into the mountains of northern Laos. Experience the thrilling ascent of Phakao Peak, sleep out beneath the twinkling universe, row across the pristine Nam Ou River, and explore small tribal villages nestled among towering karst hills.

Quick Info

  • Duration: 2 Days, 1 Night
  • Location: Nong Khiaw & Muang Ngoi
  • Rating: 4.6 / 5 (Adventure Rating)

What to Bring

👟 Sturdy hiking shoes
🧥 Warm clothing for night
🔦 Flashlight / Headlamp
📷 Camera & Powerbank

Included

  • 3 hr mountain trek to Phakao Peak
  • Panoramic sunset & sunrise views
  • Traditional Laos dinner
  • Under the stars / tent accommodation
  • 1.5 hour traditional long boat cruise
  • Sopkong, Muang Ngoi & Cave visits
  • Lunch at That Mok waterfall
  • Kayaking on the Ou River

Excluded

  • Additional snacks or souvenirs
  • Personal medical insurance
  • Personal / Hotel expenditures before check-in

Full Itinerary

Day 1: 14:00 PM - Night

Trekking & Camping Adventure

14:00 PM: Depart from Nong Khiaw.
14:15 PM: Begin a 3-hour trek through lush jungle trails to reach 360 Phakao Peak.
17:30 PM: Watch the sunset over the mountain range.
19:00 PM: Indulge in a fresh Laotian dinner.
Night: Camp under the stars in a mountain tent.

Day 2: 06:00 AM - 16:30 PM

River Cruising, Villages & Kayaking

06:00 AM: Sunrise over the sea of clouds, followed by breakfast.
09:30 AM: Descend back to Nong Khiaw.
10:00 AM: Long-tail boat up the Nam Ou River to Muang Ngoi.
13:00 PM: Boat to Sopkong Village, trek to That Mok waterfall for lunch.
15:30 PM: Kayak down the spectacular Ou River.
16:30 PM: Return to Nong Khiaw.
